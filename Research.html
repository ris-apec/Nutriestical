Our purpose stems from our research topic.
"An analysis of the nutritional value of foods consumed and how it affects the academic performance of a student."


Varying types of food can impact brain power in different ways, for example, plant-based food can help cognitive function through its nutrients like vitamin K, lutein, folate, and beta carotene, (1) or in another example, sugary drinks can cause both rational thinking and memory decline because of HFCS, high-fructose corn syrup, where in high intakes can cause negative effects on the hippocampus, a part of a brain that controls learning abilities and memory. (2) Despite healthy nutrients like protein increasing alertness, productivity, and focus, (3) an overnutrition of animal protein can be linked to anxiety and depression. (4) Both of these can decrease critical thinking and motivation in a student. (5)

Plant based foods
Fruits, vegetables, whole grains, nuts, seeds, and legumes. — By emphasizing fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes, plant-based eating can lead to increased longevity and overall well-being.

Sugary drinks
Soda, sports drinks, sweetened teas, and energy drinks — They are a significant source of "empty" calories and added sugar.

Animal protein
Meat, poultry, fish, eggs, and dairy — providing all nine essential amino acids the body needs.
